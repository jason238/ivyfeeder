About IvyFeeder

Founded in Ottawa, Ontario—the capital of Canada—in 2023, IvyFeeder Inc. supports schools, partners, and organizations with education services and software built for real-world operations.

Our team focuses on practical outcomes: stronger programs, clearer pathways for international students, and digital tools that teams can maintain and trust.

Whether you need strategic guidance, hands-on training, or a product engineered to your requirements, we work as an extension of your mission—not a one-size template.

Services
