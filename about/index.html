Who we are

We are experienced engineers with deep expertise in both software and hardware. Our background spans silicon, embedded systems, networking, and application architecture. This unique cross-disciplinary experience allows us to build products that are as technically robust as they are practically intuitive.

Tech for Real Life

IvyFeeder is a tech studio based in Ottawa, Canada. We build AI-powered apps and digital solutions designed for everyday life—from mobile applications to integrated platform systems.

How we build

We prioritize simplicity and efficiency. By integrating modern AI and connected services, we strip away unnecessary complexity to create tools that just work. Our focus is clear: improving productivity and simplifying workflows through thoughtful, digital-first engineering.
