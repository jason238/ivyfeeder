About us

IvyFeeder is a small technology team based in Ottawa, Canada.

We build practical apps and digital solutions designed to be simple, reliable, and useful for everyday life and business. From mobile and web applications to larger platform-based systems currently in development, our goal is to create technology people genuinely enjoy using.

As technology continues to evolve, we are also exploring AI-powered capabilities and intelligent workflows in our newer solutions.

Who we are

We are developers, engineers, and makers with years of experience in both software and hardware development.

Our background includes application development, embedded systems, automation, APIs, and product design. This combination of experience allows us to approach projects from both technical and practical perspectives.

We care about building products that are stable, intuitive, and designed for long-term value.

How we build

We believe good technology should feel simple.

That means building products that are clean, efficient, and thoughtfully designed — without unnecessary complexity. Some of our applications already include AI-assisted features, while newer projects are being developed with deeper integration of modern AI technologies and connected services.

We are especially interested in using technology to improve productivity, simplify workflows, and create better digital experiences.
