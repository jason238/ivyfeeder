Who we are

We’re a small team—just a handful of developers who love turning ideas into apps people can hold in their hands. We’re not chasing hype; we care about useful, well-made mobile software that respects your time and your privacy. This site is our home base: a quiet corner of the internet where we share what we build and write almost daily on each app’s blog.

How we build

Our development philosophy is simple: ship often, listen closely, and polish the details. We iterate in short loops, keep our stack boring where it helps (hello, static sites and Markdown), and treat every review and email as a chance to improve. We believe small teams can punch above their weight when they stay curious, honest, and a little bit stubborn about quality.
