Welcome to {{ page.app_name }} (“we”, “our”, or “the App”).

Time Coin is designed to help users build healthier focus habits through positive reinforcement and gamified rewards.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Time Coin handles information when you use the App.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Time Coin does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information from users.

We do not require:

  • Account registration
  • Email sign-up
  • Phone number verification
  • Cloud synchronization

All app data is stored locally on the user’s device only.

2. Local Device Storage

Time Coin stores app-related information locally on your device using simple device storage mechanisms such as application preferences.

Examples may include:

  • Focus timer progress
  • Earned Time Coins
  • Levels and badges
  • App settings and preferences
  • Usage statistics

This information never leaves your device unless you manually delete or back up your device using your platform provider’s services.

We do not use cloud databases or remote servers to store user data.

3. Third-Party Services

Time Coin may interact with limited third-party platform services necessary for app distribution and purchases.

These may include:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store
  • RevenueCat (subscription and purchase management)

These services may collect technical or purchase-related information according to their own privacy policies.

We do not receive or store payment card information.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

If you purchase premium features or subscriptions:

  • Payments are processed securely by Apple or Google
  • Subscription management may be handled through RevenueCat

We do not directly process payments or store billing information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Time Coin is designed for general audiences, including students and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

Because the App does not collect user data, we do not maintain databases containing children’s personal information.

6. Data Security

Since user data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to our servers, privacy risks are minimized. However, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

7. Data Deletion

Users may delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app’s storage through device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Because we do not maintain cloud databases, deleted local data cannot be recovered by us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
