Overview

{{ page.app_name }} is designed to help parents manage emotional stress through guided breathing exercises, calming prompts, and positive progress tracking.

We designed the app with a minimal-data philosophy:

  • No account is required.
  • No sensitive parenting data is uploaded.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The app may store limited information locally on your device, such as:

  • Your successful calm session count
  • App preferences and settings
  • Progress or achievement records

This information is stored locally unless cloud backup is enabled by your device platform.

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Anonymous usage analytics

This information helps us improve app stability and performance.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Maintain app performance and security
  • Fix crashes and technical issues
  • Understand general usage trends
  • Improve the user experience

We do not use your information for behavioral advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited technical data with trusted service providers that help us operate the app, such as:

  • Analytics providers
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud infrastructure providers

These providers may only access information necessary to perform services on our behalf.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for use by parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention

Because most app data is stored locally on your device, you may remove your information at any time by:

  • Clearing app data
  • Uninstalling the app

Any diagnostic or analytics data retained by our service providers is kept only as long as reasonably necessary.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and keep the app secure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reporting tools

Please review the privacy policies of those providers for additional information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your information
  • Request deletion of data
  • Restrict certain processing activities

You may contact us regarding any privacy-related requests.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted within the app or on our website with a revised “Last updated” date.
