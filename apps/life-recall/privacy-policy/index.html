Welcome to Life Recall (“Life Recall”, “we”, “our”, or “the App”).

Life Recall is an AI-powered memoir app that helps users preserve personal memories and life stories through guided conversations and AI-generated autobiographies.

Your privacy and personal stories are deeply important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of Life Recall, we may collect the following types of information:

A. Profile Information

Information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name or nickname
  • Birth year
  • Hometown
  • Occupation
  • Family or personal background information
  • Memoir preferences

B. Conversation Content

Life Recall stores conversations between you and the AI memoir assistant, including:

  • Text conversations
  • Voice recordings (if voice features are enabled)
  • Speech-to-text transcriptions
  • AI-generated memoir content
  • Generated autobiography chapters and PDFs

This information is necessary for generating and improving your memoir experience.

C. Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous identifiers
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Usage analytics

D. Purchase Information

If you purchase subscriptions or premium features, payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

Subscription management may be processed through third-party providers such as RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI conversation and memoir generation features
  • Save and organize memoir content across sessions
  • Generate downloadable PDF autobiographies
  • Improve AI conversation quality and user experience
  • Maintain app performance and security
  • Analyze app usage trends
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscriptions and purchases

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers.

3. AI & Third-Party Services

Life Recall uses third-party AI and cloud services to provide core functionality. These services may process user content temporarily to generate responses and memoir content.

Third-party providers may include:

  • OpenAI (AI conversation and memoir generation)
  • OpenAI TTS (speech recognition and text-to-speech)
  • Firebase (authentication, analytics, diagnostics)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Conversation history and memoir content may be stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In secure cloud services used by the App

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service and improve the App.

You may request deletion of your account data and memoir content by contacting us.

5. Voice Data

If voice features are enabled:

  • Voice input may be temporarily processed by third-party speech recognition services to convert speech into text. We do not permanently store voice recordings.
  • Voice data is used only to provide transcription and conversation functionality.
  • We do not use voice recordings for advertising purposes.

6. PDF Export & User Content

Life Recall allows users to generate downloadable memoir PDFs based on conversation history. Users are responsible for reviewing generated content before sharing or printing.

We do not publicly publish memoir content without user consent.

7. Children’s Privacy

Life Recall is intended for adults and family use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old (or the minimum age required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information, including:

  • Secure data transmission
  • Access controls
  • Cloud security protections

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

You may contact us regarding privacy-related requests.

10. International Data Transfers & Processing

To provide our AI-driven services, your information is processed and stored on secure servers located in the United States. We partner with industry-leading providers (such as OpenAI and ElevenLabs) that maintain rigorous security standards. By using the App, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be posted within the App or on our website with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
